Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?

The timeline depends on the scope of your project. Basic updates often require 1'2 weeks, but full remodels including custom features typically take 3'5 weeks. We always provide a clear schedule before starting, and we keep you updated every step of the way.

Do you offer free consultations or estimates?

Yes! We offer free, no-obligation consultations to understand your vision and provide a detailed estimate based on your needs, design preferences, and budget.

Can you help me design my bathroom, or do I need to hire a separate designer?

Absolutely'we offer complete design assistance. From layout planning to selecting tiles, fixtures, and finishes, we'll help you create a beautiful space that reflects your style and meets your functionality needs.

What types of tile and flooring do you install?

From ceramic and porcelain to marble, travertine, mosaic, and luxury vinyl tile (LVT), we install it all and assist you in picking the best material based on durability, moisture protection, and style.

Will I need to leave my home during the remodel?

In most cases, you can stay in your home. We take every precaution to minimize disruption, keep the workspace clean, and maintain safety. For full remodels with plumbing or electrical work, we'll coordinate with you if temporary adjustments are needed.

Are your remodels waterproofed and built to code?

Definitely. Our team adheres to Chetek, Wisconsin building codes and employs industry-leading waterproofing practices in wet zones like showers and flooring to ensure long-term prevention of mold, leaks, and water damage.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, all of our remodeling services come with a satisfaction guarantee and a workmanship warranty. Specific warranties vary by material and service, and we'll go over all the details before we begin work.

Can you work within my budget?

Certainly. No matter if you want a premium renovation or a cost-effective refresh, we adapt our services to your budget while ensuring excellent results and style.
